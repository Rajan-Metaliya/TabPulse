Custom URL Patterns

Add your company-specific URLs. Use * as wildcard. The extension will attempt to detect Jira/Bitbucket-like patterns automatically.

Examples:

https://your-company.atlassian.net/browse/* https://bitbucket.your-org.com/*/pull-requests/* https://jira.company.com/browse/*
